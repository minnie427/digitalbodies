Welcome to our project

This project is a curation of interactive 3D art pieces exploring the theme of human bodies extending into the digital environment. Our works are designed utilising TouchDesigner as the main software. The curation will feature each of our works separately, along with a collaborative piece on the main screen, accompanied by music designed specifically for this project.
